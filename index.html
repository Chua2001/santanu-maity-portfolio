About

Crafting expressive paintings that speak to the heart.

Santanu Maity is a freelance artist with over 25 years of experience specializing in oil painting, sketching, abstract art, watercolor techniques and brush work. His work blends disciplined technique with emotional depth, creating pieces that resonate across cultural and artistic boundaries.

Specializing in commissioned paintings, workshops, exhibitions, and mentoring emerging artists with a premium creative approach.
